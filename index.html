What is Blue Economy?
Blue economy is a term in economics
relating to the exploitation and
preservation of the marine environment.
Its scope of interpretation varies among
organizations.
According to the World Bank, the blue
economy is the "sustainable use of ocean
resources for economic growth, improved
livelihoods, and jobs while preserving
the health of ocean ecosystem."
