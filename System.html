What si the sistem

System 31 is very efficient and useful.
It consists of 3filters, each refined by
something. For example, the first purifies
from large waste materials, the second
from the smaller ones that have passed,
the second filter can also purify some
harmful substances such as chlorine.
The third filter will purify water
from all residual materials and
harmful substances that have not
been purified in the previous
two filters.
System 31 will have the function
of purifying contaminated water
from harmful particles that have
entered it. The end result of
all this will be purified water.
